This is the drawer where I keep ideas — some fully formed, some half-baked, some just a sentence.

Projects, experiments, software, website concepts, time capsules, and random things worth building someday.

### Why keep an ideas drawer?

Ideas are fragile. They need a place to rest before they're ready. Some will grow into projects; others will quietly wait. Writing them down is the first step.
