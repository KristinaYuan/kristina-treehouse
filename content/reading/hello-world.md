Reading is one of the great pleasures of life. But without notes, the details fade.

Here I'll keep notes on books, papers, films, music, and essays — not full summaries, but the parts that stayed with me, the ideas that changed how I think.

### What to expect

- Book notes and reflections
- Paper summaries
- Film and music reviews
- Links to essays worth reading
