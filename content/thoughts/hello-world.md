Some thoughts take time to form. They don't fit neatly into a knowledge base or a story. They're slower, more personal — reflections on time, memory, growth, identity, and the small textures of everyday life.

This section is for those thoughts. Personal essays, philosophy, meditations. Writing them down is a way of paying attention to what matters.
